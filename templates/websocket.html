About WebSocket XSS

WebSocket XSS occurs when the application fails to properly sanitize messages received via WebSocket before rendering them in the DOM.

Exploitation Tips

  • Messages are often directly inserted into the DOM
  • Look for event handlers or JavaScript execution contexts
  • Try persistent payloads that affect other users
  • Combine with other attack vectors like CSRF
